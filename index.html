Southern Warmth & Cool Vibes

Welcome to our modern haven centrally poised amid the charms of Wilmington, an alluring coastal North Carolina town. Hotel Lela places you just four miles from Wilmington’s Historic Riverfront District, six miles from scenic Wrightsville Beach, and two miles from UNCW.

The name Lela evokes a free-spirited nature, a sense of grace, and an appreciation for art and beauty. No other name better captures what Hotel Lela offers. Enjoy a serene aesthetic with vibrant pops of color, an outdoor saltwater pool surrounded by cabanas, two golf swing suites, a standout signature restaurant, and all the warmth this historic yet progressive town is known for.

Gracious Guest Rooms

Awash in soothing creams and neutrals, our guest rooms envelope you in contemporary comfort. From spacious desks to built-in seating nooks, we’ve designed a serene ambience to make your stay worth texting home about.

Swing Suites

Our Swing Suites are where eagles, birdies, and good times happen. Try your skills at bocce ball out by the pool or get in some reps at the Fitness Center. When evening comes, reflect on your awesome day in the glow of our firepit.

Curated Flavors

Our signature restaurant, Paloma’s at Lela, tempts your palate with bold Latin flavors paired with artisanal spirits. If you’re in the mood for alfresco cocktails, wine, beer, and snacks, step outside to the Pool Bar.
